Explorations · 5 themes × 5 screens

Twenty-five ways this app could look.

Five complete directions, each a distinct palette and a distinct layout structure, drawn across the same five screens. Every screen shows the real interactions the Composio research confirmed we can ship: approve and comment on GitHub, reply and react in Slack, mark read, snooze, open in browser. Pick one row and we unify everything to it.

01

Graphite & Gold

Inky graphite with restrained metallic gold. Premium through contrast and scarcity of colour, not brightness. Layout: dense list, grouped clusters, hairline rows. The most information per screen, best for power users.
